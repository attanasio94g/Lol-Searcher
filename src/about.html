About the extension
This is an unofficial extensions for League of Legends developed to improve myself and to do some experience. Actually the extension is very simple, you just need to insert your username and server and when you click on the icon the browser will create a page of the selected site with your account. I spend a bit of my time to make this and I hope to improve the extensions in the future.
