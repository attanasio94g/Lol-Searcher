About the extension

This is an unofficial extensions of Op.gg site developed to improve myself and to do some experience. Actually the extension is very simple, you just need to insert your username and server and when you click on the icon the browser will create a page of Op.gg with your account. I spend a bit of my time to make this and I hope to improve the extensions in the future.
